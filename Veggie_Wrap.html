Veggie Wrap

Home

Description

This Rainbow Veggie Wrap is a fresh, portable meal full of crunchy vegetables and plant-based protein. It’s perfect for a light lunch or snack and packed with vitamins, fiber, and flavor.

With a colorful combination of hummus, chickpeas, and vibrant veggies, it’s healthy, satisfying, and visually stunning—great for a quick meal or taking on the go.

Ingredients

  • 1 large whole-grain wrap or tortilla
  • 2 tablespoons hummus
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers (red, yellow, green)
  • 1/4 cup cucumber slices
  • Sprouts (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Lay the wrap flat on a clean surface.
  2. Spread hummus evenly over the surface of the wrap.
  3. Layer chickpeas, shredded carrots, bell peppers, cucumber, and greens evenly.
  4. Add optional sprouts for extra crunch and nutrition.
  5. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
  6. Carefully roll the wrap tightly from one end to the other.
  7. Slice in half and serve immediately or wrap in foil for later.
