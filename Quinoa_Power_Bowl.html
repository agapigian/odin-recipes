Quinoa Power Bowl

Home

Description

This Quinoa Power Bowl is a vibrant, nutritious meal packed with plant-based proteins, fresh vegetables, and whole grains. It’s perfect for a healthy lunch or dinner and keeps you energized throughout the day.

With a zesty lime-tahini dressing and colorful veggies, it’s not only delicious but also visually appealing—an easy way to eat clean without sacrificing flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup black beans, cooked or canned (rinsed)
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup diced cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: fresh cilantro or parsley

Steps

  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions and set aside to cool slightly.
  2. Prepare black beans (rinse if canned) and corn (steam or microwave briefly).
  3. Dice cherry tomatoes, shred carrots, and slice avocado.
  4. Whisk together tahini, lime juice, salt, and a little water to make the dressing.
  5. Assemble the bowl: place quinoa as the base, then layer black beans, corn, tomatoes, carrots, and avocado on top.
  6. Drizzle with the lime-tahini dressing and sprinkle fresh herbs if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your colorful power bowl.
